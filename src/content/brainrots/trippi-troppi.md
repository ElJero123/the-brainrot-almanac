        Trippi Troppi is a bear who has the head of a fish. He is a popular Italian brainrot character. He is often depicted as a shrimp who is mixed with a cat. Video by TikTok user @1raidex. Although the shrimp version is not the original, it is more popular. The shrimp-cat Trippi Troppi is actually siblings with another brainrot Trulimero Trulichina(the cat variant). this brainrot is also the component of Trippa Troppa Tralala Lirilìrà Tung Tung Sahur Boneca Tung Tung Tralalelo Trippi Troppa Crocodina.

Aunque la versión de camarón no es la original, es más popular. El camarón-gato Trippi Troppi es en realidad hermano de otro brainrot, Trulimero Trulichina (la variante felina).

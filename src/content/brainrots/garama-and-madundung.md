        Garamararambraramanmararaman dan Madudungdungdung Tak Tuntung Perkuntung (Popularly known just as "Garamararam" or "Garama and Madungdung" or "Garam dan Madu") is an anthropomorphic Italian brainrot character, comprising of a salt shaker and a honey jar, with humanlike faces and humanlike hairy legs.

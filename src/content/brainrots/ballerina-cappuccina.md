history:
    english: |
        Ballerina Cappuccina is an Italian brainrot character introduced on the 19th of March, 2025 by TikTok user @aironicfun. She is a human ballerina with a coffee mug as her head. She is the wife of Cappuccino Assassino. She can usually be seen dancing or singing. In an older iteration of the meme, she could be seen pirouetting gracefully. She is one of the first Italian brainrots.

personality:
    english: |
        Her rival is Espressona Signora, who seduced her husband, Cappuccino Assassino.
        Despite being married, she is often depicted in love with (cheating with) Tung Tung Tung Sahur. This doesn't count as something canon since her creator didn't pair her with him, but with her husband, Capuccino Assasino.
        Both Ballerina Nicotina and Icharinna Matchanova have a rivalry with Ballerina Cappuccina although these two said rivalries seem to be one sided.
        There are a few Candle Cove headcanons based around the idea that the show's world is the same as Italian Brainrot's universe with one said fan headcanons being that Ballerina Cappuccina is the secret identity of Hoody Hans (a villain in the show's first season) via some The Mask style shenanigans involving "his" yellow cloak.
    spanish: |
        Su rival es Espressona Signora, quien sedujo a su esposo, Cappuccino Assassino.  
        A pesar de estar casada, a menudo se la representa enamorada (engañando) con Tung Tung Tung Sahur. Esto no cuenta como algo canónico, ya que su creador no la emparejó con él, sino con su esposo, Cappuccino Assassino.  
        Tanto Ballerina Nicotina como Icharinna Matchanova tienen una rivalidad con Ballerina Cappuccina, aunque ambas rivalidades parecen ser unilaterales.  
        Existen algunos headcanons de *Candle Cove* basados en la idea de que el mundo del show es el mismo que el del universo de Italian Brainrot, con uno de esos headcanons diciendo que Ballerina Cappuccina es la identidad secreta de Hoody Hans (un villano de la primera temporada del show), a través de un truco al estilo de *La Máscara* que involucra su capa amarilla.

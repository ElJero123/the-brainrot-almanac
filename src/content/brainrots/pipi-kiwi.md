personality:
    english: |
        Pipi Kiwi is characterized by its raccoon head and striped tailas well as its slightly unoticeable furry raccoon legs, a human's feet, and a kiwi's body. The raccoon head and tail both come from the center of the kiwi known as the placenta, the original version does not include the neck or arms, but the secondary alternative version includes more features of a raccon like the leg and necks. The kiwi is cut in half revealing the insides of the kiwi, but the skin of the kiwi still remains. This is a large contrast from the character Pipi Kiwi from the book the brainrot Pipi Kiwi is based off of, in the book Pipi Kiwi is a small Kiwi bird, while in Italian Brainrot, Pipi Kiwi is a raccoon-kiwi mammal hybrid.
